My Professional Journey 📈

This is a glimpse into my daily life and experiences during my time at the shop.

---

🌅 Day-to-Day Tasks

My workdays were long and packed! Here's how a typical day unfolded:

  • ⏰ **7 AM:** Shop opening and arrival. Often on an empty stomach! 🍽️
  • 👥 **7 AM - 10 AM:** Cashier supervision until the manager arrived.
  • 💻 **10 AM onwards:** Back office tasks would begin in earnest:
    • 📦 Goods Received Notes (GRN)
    • 💸 Accounts Payable
    • 🧾 Accounts Receivable
    • 🏦 Bank Reconciliation
    • 🛍️ Placing Orders
  • 🗣️ Transitioning between back office and cashier duties.
  • ❓ Handling customer inquiries.
  • 🍽️ **2 PM:** Lunch would usually arrive. Struggled a lot with gastritis; my diet was mainly rice and ugali, no beef, no tea. 🍚
  • 🌙 **Afternoon till 9 PM:** Afternoon tasks continued until shop closing.
  • 🗓️ I had Sundays off.
---

✨ Benefits I Gained

Despite the challenges, there were some definite positives:

  • 💰 **Earnings:** Up to 2025, I earned a gross of KSh 38,000 and a net of KSh 34,000.
  • 💡 Utilizing my skills to their fullest.
  • 🕊️ Freedom from constant senior supervision.
  • 🤝 Benefits from the trust I earned.
