My Health Journey 💔

This section details the significant health challenges I faced during this period, often impacting my daily life and finances.

---

🩺 Frequent Health Issues

Month after month, I encountered various health problems. I attributed a large part of these challenges to "witchcraft diet aspects," which made matters worse. 😔

  • 🩸 **Blood Infection:** A recurring issue that caused much discomfort.
  • 🚽 **Urinary Infection:** Another frequent and painful ailment.
  • 🦟 **Malaria:** A common and debilitating illness in the region.
  • 🤢 **Typhoid:** An severe bacterial infection that hit hard.
  • 💥 **Gastritis:** This was the **mother of all** my health struggles, truly a persistent and draining condition.

On average, I roughly spent KSh 4,000 a month on hospital visits and medication, which was a significant financial burden. 💸

---

😩 The Battle with Gastritis

Gastritis was by far my toughest health battle. I really struggled with it due to the lack of a proper, varied diet. My weight significantly reduced because of the limited food intake. 😔

My diet was extremely restrictive, mainly consisting of:

  • 🍚 Ugali and Cabbage
  • 🥬 Rice and Cabbage

There was practically nothing else I could eat without aggravating my condition. I spent a considerable amount of money on various medications trying to manage the pain and symptoms, but it remained a persistent and difficult struggle. 💊
